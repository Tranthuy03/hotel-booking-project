ABOUT US

ROYAL HOTEL is a 5-star luxury resort located on the pristine shores of Bai Khem, Phu Quoc. Designed by renowned architect Bill Bensley, the resort features a whimsical concept inspired by a fictional 19th-century French university. With 234 stylish rooms and villas, world-class dining, a signature spa, and breathtaking beachfront views.
